Research Experience

Papers

  • Measurement based dimension descent association algorithm for OTHR multi-detection multi-target tracking

ISA Transactions (2023)

Based on the characteristics of multi-model detection of OTHR radar, this article decomposes the multi-target state estimation problem into two correlation tasks, namely measurement-detection model association and measurement-target data association. The measurements and detection models are associated through the mapping relationship between the measurement spaces and the state space, and then the probabilistic hypothesis density algorithm is used to perform the measurement-target association and estimate the multi-target state.

  • SOM Neural Network Based Gaussian Mixture PHD Algorithm for Multi-Sensor Multi-Target Tracking

ICGNC (2022)

The Gaussian mixture probability hypothesis density algorithm requires a large amount of computing resources in the fusion and pruning step, especially when the number of clutter and targets is large. The SOM network is used to cluster Gaussian components to reduce calculations. When performing distributed fusion, the SOM network is also used to cluster Gaussian components from different sensors to achieve distributed fusion state estimation.

  • Research on distributed multi-target robust tracking method

Master's Thesis (2021-2024)

My master's thesis tries to solve the problem of robust multi-target tracking with decreased detection precision and limited communication. For the problem that the precision of some sensors in the wireless sensor network decreases due to interference or enemy spies, how to judge the nodes with decreased precision is essential. In addition, how to reduce or even eliminate the impact of these precision-decreased nodes on the target state estimation is also worth thinking about. To deal with these two challenges, I combined the clustering algorithm with filtering methods to infer the nodes with decreased precision, and add an initialization step to the consensus fusion, introducing an initial weight matrix to remove the local estimations of sensors with decreased precision. In order to reduce the communication burden, I design an event trigger algorithm that takes the current filtering state as the trigger condition. The trigger threshold can be adjusted according to the required accuracy of state estimation.

Projects

  • Research on collaborative state estimation technology (2021-2023)

For the complex dynamic environment affected by multiple interference factors, the target detection information obtained by each sensor is incomplete, or the communication topology relationship between sensors is switched, etc., a distributed collaborative state estimation technology is developed to realize information Coordinated state estimation under incomplete conditions.

  • Guidance control-oriented offensive and defensive confrontation model analysis and strategy research (2022 - present)

For the problem of threat identification, first build a model of enemy target (FEKO and SolidWorks) to obtain a small sample data set, and then use the transfer learning method to train the corresponding target recognition network to achieve identification tasks under small sample conditions. Considering that there are multiple threats in the scene, we need to evaluate the identified threats. The target state estimation technology is used to predict the movement of the threat targets, determine their intentions, and estimate strategies.

  • Research on intelligent decision-making algorithm for high-value target attack (2022 - present)

First, anti-damage models of typical targets and key parts are constructed, and multiple anti-damage capability models based on the characteristics of different targets and key parts are built. Then, the proportion method is used to divide the key parts of typical targets, and we use the static Bayesian network and the constructed anti-damage capability model to predict the threat levels of the targets and key parts. Finally, the complex network strike chain method is used to construct a strike preparation chain and strike execution chain in the scenario to evaluate the value of typical targets and key parts based on the complexity of the strike chain.
